About My Site

My proposal for this site is a website for a fictional recording studio called Drop D Records. The purpose of the site will be to inform any potential clients of what services the company offers and how you can find out more.

List of content pages:

-Home page
The main page with snippets of information and links to the other pages on the site.
-About Us
A smaller page with more background about the company.
-Our Facility
A page describing the quality of our studio and equipment.
-Our Services
A brief decription of what services are offered, along with pricing.
-Contact Us
A page with our address,phone number, etc., along with a form that can be filled out that will request more information via email.
