About My Site

My proposal for this site is a website for a fictional recording studio called Drop D Records. The purpose of the site will be to inform any potential clients of what services the company offers and how you can find out more.

Target Audience

The target audience for this site is any musicians who do not have a place to record their music. We want customers who will be willing to come any and record their tracks.

Persona

Ben

Ben is a guitar player who has written a few songs. He has recorded some rough takes of his songs with his laptop, but he would like a place where he can record more professional sounding tracks. He is looking for a place with state-of-the-art equipment and people who can help him make his music sound great. He is also looking for a professional looking company instead of some hole-in-the-wall place.
